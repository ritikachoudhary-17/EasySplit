How to Use:


  1. First of all register yourself. Click on SignUp and enter your good name with an email id and password. After that sign in with that email id and password.



  2. Enter the name of trip, holiday or destination you want to split the bills in the box quoted as "Enter Trip Name" and press "Get Started" button.



  3. Now enter the details of all persons like their name, UPI Id, purpose of expenditure and amount.



  4. After calculating the result will be shown as follows. If you click on "Show UPI Id", it will show you UPI Id of person to whom you have to pay.



  5. Just above the trip name, download and save button appear after calculation. You can download the result as pdf and you can save this calculation by clicking on save icon.



  6. The pdf will be shown as:



  7. If you wish to restart the process, simply click on the logo of "Easy Split" at the top-left corner in navigation bar. It will redirect you to the main page.



  8. If you want to know about the website, you can click on "About" button in navigation bar. To learn how to use the website, click on "How to use" button in navigation panel. To contact us, click on "Contact" button.
