What you get?

Everyone pays something for a group of people on vacations, for gifts, at parties and so on. Even with a calculator, calculating the payments afterwards to balance out the group can be a challenging and difficult task, especially when some expenses are not shared by all group members.
Easy Split helps you with that: After you entered everyone's name and the spendings she or he had for the group, the Easy Split calculator shows you who owes whom how much and how to settle debts in the group.
